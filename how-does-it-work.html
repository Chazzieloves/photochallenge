Participate in the challenge!

Prepare

Every month a new challenge is released! Every challenge has a new topic, prepare to wow us with your photos. To get the odds in your favor you should think about how your photo/photos relate to the challenge topic.

Magic

Work your magic! Go out or stay inside. Once you have a clear vision of what you want to portray for the challenge, create! It does not matter if you are a proffessional or newbie photographer. If you have a camera or phone, this is a creative challenge and the content is more important than the resolution of the photo.

Submit

You have until the 15th day of the month to fill out the form and participate. We usually need 1 week to look through all photos from participants, all participants will be notified if any of their submitted photos has won latest the 22th day of the month. The winner sends us the photos they want us to expose. The 1 day of the month we display the new challenge winner and reveal next months challenge.
